## [Tiffhax](https://github.com/emilyselwood/tiffhax)

A handy tool to view the internals of a tiff file. Useful when trying to build something that edits or works with tiff files on a byte level.

![A screen shot of tiff hax, a html table with colourful bits highlighted](https://github.com/emilyselwood/tiffhax/blob/master/screenshot.png?raw=true)
